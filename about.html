ABOUT SHELL TECHNOLOGIES

Shell Technologies is a technology solutions provider based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are specialized in customized Software Development, System Integration and providing competitive Outsourcing Services to the clients from all over the world.

Our Mission

To maximize the value of our services by providing solutions based on professionalism, transparency, trust, and maximum return on investment for both of our clients and employees.

Our Vision

To be the best technology solutions provider in South Asia by fully unleashing the potential of the Bangladeshi talents on the world stage and by doing so, we aim to make a positive contribution to the developing nation.

Why Shell Technologies

Talent Pool - Our Technology Services team and Project Management team are both comprised of versatile, highly skilled and highly experienced individuals who can ensure the deliverables you wish to receive on time. We also have an extensive database of highly qualified consultants for project based assignments. Cost - Our competitive advantages are our quality human resources and infrastructural facilities at a cost far less than their international equivalents. The economy of Bangladesh supports better lifestyle in less expense compared to our neighbors. That attracts the increasing number of talents graduating in IT and Fine Arts, who are our obvious pick. Scalability - Our staffing plan supports variable number of resource engagement within short notices. Started with a small project with us, many of our customers extended their contracts to engage additional resources for larger projects. Project Management - In addition to our own project management capabilities, we establish reporting channels to the customers for parallel reporting on progress. This helps you to communicate and monitor the projects directly and ensure quality output and timely delivery of all requirements.
